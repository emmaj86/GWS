About us

Groundworks Scotland Ltd was created by Sean Jamieson & Kevin Campbell who combined have over 30 years experience within the construction industry.

Both started their careers learning on the tools and through hard work and determination climbed the construction ladder that has seen them take on foreman, site engineering, site management and in the latter contract management positions with various construction companies in Scotland.

Our goal for this business is to show by working hard and always digging deep we will create client foundations based on our mindset and in turn being the first choice groundworks contractor in Scotland.
